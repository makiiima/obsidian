## 3 The Stack ADT
### ADT
- LIFO: Last-In-First-Out
- Insertions and deletions at the **top** only
- Objects: A finite orderd list with zero or more elements
- Operations:
	- Int IsEmpty(Stack S)
	- Stack CreateStack()
	- DisposeStack(Stack S)
	- MakeEmpty(Stack S)
	- Push(ElementType X, Stack S)
	- ELementType Top(Stack S)
	- Pop(Stack S)
